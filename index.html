1.Chocolate Chip Cookies

Everyone's favorite homemade treat!

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 12 mins
Serves: 4–6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix until smooth.
  4. Mix dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  5. Combine all and stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto baking tray and bake for 10–12 mins.
Tip: For chewy cookies, take them out right before they look fully done.

Nutritional Info

Nutrient Per Cookie
Calories 120 kcal
Carbohydrates 16 g
Fat 6 g

Glossary

Cream
- To beat ingredients until smooth and fluffy
Bake
- Cook with dry heat in an oven
